About Me

Hello! As you may have guessed by the header, I'm Aimee Shields, a Game Developer and programmer based out of Ottawa, ON. I graduated from Algonquin College's Game Development program in 2026, but I've been programming and making art a lot longer than that! I completed the Drawing Foundations for Animation and Illustration in 2023, and was at University of Guelph for a BaSc. in Computer Sciences and Media Studies from 2019-2022 before that.

I've always liked solving problems, and the fun in writing code is the same as it is in a good puzzle. I like tinkering with complex systems and finding weird solutions to weird problems, and that's a big part of why I like making games!

I also draw and 3D model, as well as working with traditional mediums like india ink and graphite.

I'm always up to chat, so if there's a part of my work that interests you or a project of your own that you think I'd like to see, reach out and let me know.

Thanks for stopping by!
